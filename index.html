Mangata & Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries. The jewelry company primarily operates online and has a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier attached for browsing. Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look.

Mariana is the owner and the lead designer of Mangata & Gallo. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened a store in her hometown, Austin and started to grow her business online.
